ExoPlaneteer Overview ExoPlaneteer is a casual game for busy adults who cannot sit in front of screen playing games all day long. The game play is simple: Played on a desktop, cellphone or tablet, the game is a small web application where players buy one or more planet NFTs anytime they want. Each hour, events or opportunties will occur on the planet and the player can respond to these with a simple yes or no or spending some planetary assets to influence the outcome of the opportunity: Examples: Your civilization is becoming totalitarian, spend money to counteract this trend? fWriting is invented by a clever individual, do you want to help it spread or suppress it? See the video showing the game play... The reward for making correct decisions is to raise the technology level and population of the planet. The higher each is, the more reward is given each hour. The reward is given in Tritanium-44 Planetary Treasury assets, which can later be swapped for actual, on chain cryptocurrency whenever the player wants. Each planet would have its own events and opportunities. The frequency and type of these opportunities is determined Each planet has natural resources which can be combined with other resources to build infrastructure, goods and services. Normally this will require trading with other players to aquire the necessary ingredients for more advanced products. Exchange contracts can be agreed upon between planets where each hour a set amount of resources and Tritanium-44 are swapped in a continuous supply change. The separate (in light years) between planets determines how long it takes fo such goods to arrive. It is better to aquire multiple planets within the same solar system (same star) because commerce between them is instant, no waiting. Online game involving buying, selling, and developing exoplanets and their civilizations to earn cryptocurrency. Each planet is a set of a video file of the revolving planet, image of the dominant, indigenous, intelligent life form, and a description of their culture and history. Planets are each onchain artwork NFTs that the player owns outright. He sell the planet, develop the planet and engage in commerce with other planets to speed up development. The planets are arranged by solar system and the manager. Planets earn a cryptocurrency based upon their population and technology level. The game play consists of receiving hourly events and responding to them. The events represent points of opportunity or crisis that the player must decide how to deal with. This is not the game for hard core gamers, it's for casual players who cannot, or do not want to, sit in front of a screen in all their free time. It's also probably 1/100th of the effort required for a full blown, immersion, space shooter. This is more like buying and managing fictional, galactic real estate. Planets are NFTs Planets acquire crypto token Titanium-44 Planets actively acquire crypto and can be interacted with for entertainment Simple game for underserved market of consumers with disposable incomes who don't have time to sit in front of computer playing games all day long. This offers a diversion and entertainment without monopolizing ones time .
