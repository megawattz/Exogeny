Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Exogeny?
A: Exogeny is the game where you get to own and manage entire planets, virtually of course. The planets you own and manage are NFTs which you outright own. The mp4 and images of the planet are yours. You can buy and sell entire planets if you want. However, the metatdata about the civilization on that planet is controlled by the Exogeny game engine.

Q: Why should I care about the civilization on the planet?
A: Because the civilization is what produces Tritanium-44. The better the civilization, the more Tritanium-44 you get deposited into your wallet. No civilization = No Tritanium-44

Q: What is Tritanium-44 (T44)
A: Tritanium-44 is the reward of a well managed planet. Tritanium-44 is a typical ERC-20 token tradeable on DEXes, exchangeable for any other supported blockchain crypto (Ethererum, Binance, Polygon, AVAX)

Q: What does manage entire planets mean?
A: It means you acquire planets with resources and initial life forms which you must actively manage to evolve intelligent life to gain tech levels. The higher the tech level (and other factors like population, culture, resources etc) the more Tritanium-44 each planet earns for you. You can envision yourself as the "guiding spirit" of the planet. An entity with the power to influence individuals and groups indirectly, by putting ideas and suggestions and motivations into their minds. Directly manifesting godlike powers to a population has been found to give them an inferiority complex where they stop being self motivated, so direct involvement is forbidden by the Galactic Confederation.

Q: What is a well managed planet?
A: For the purposes of the game, it's a planet that produces lots of Tritanium-44. Tritanium-44 production is best done by an energetic, enthusiastic population using high-tech methods. Forced labor and low-tech tools don't create much Tritanium-44. The game engine takes all the attributes of your planet and each hour (100 years of planet history) calculates what the Tritanium-44 production level is and credits your wallet with that much. Your job is to stimulate the population growth, tech level and morale of the civilization of the dominant, indigenous species. Different species need different types of cultures to be happy. There is no one-size fits all. Changing any of the mutable aspects of the planet requires investment at the time a chance appearance of a visionary appears.

Q: How is Tritanium-44 generated?
A: Tritanium-44 uses Proof-Of-Attention. You must actively manage your planets to grow or even sustain their Tritanium-44 production levels. If you do not, the civilizations will collapse and your planet will return to its untamed, natural state.

Q: If Exogeny controls my civilization, can it take all my Tritanium-44 back?
A: No. Tritanium-44 is a typical crypto game token governed by a smart contract which does not allow the contract owner to alter the balances of the token owners. However, the game engine is in charge of creating more supply of T44 as rewards for planets well managed.

Q: Why aren't the planets astronomicaly accurate?? Why are they are rotating so fast, and why are the moons too close to have stable orbits due to the three+ body problem?
A: It's a game. It's entertainment. We experimented wth being more accurate but a more slowly rotating planet was too uninteresting to watch. Moons spaced realistically were so far from the planet that the camera also had to be far from the entire system to fit everything into the scene, making the planet and moons tiny, and uninteresting to watch.

Q: Why do many of the planets have Indian, Egyptian, Chinese, Arabic, Nordic, Russian, whimsical and some even silly names?
A: It's imagined that the discoverer of the planet or star system gets to name it so a general lack of formality has prevailed during this era. Something like that.

Q: How is the game played?
A: Every hour the Exogeny game engine evaluates each planet. Each hour of game time is one era of planet time, roughly 100 years of Earth time. During each era, several things happen

  1. Random events will occur
    • Natural disasters or similar problems may occur. How well your planet weathers those is based upon the tech levels, population and culture. For example,
    • A plague may befall your civilization. if you have invested T44 in Bio technology, a plague will have little downside. If you do not, you could lose half your population.
    • Finding an artifact from a more advanced civilization can give your culture an instant tech level jump.
    • Often, if you have invested in technology, you may get a warning about disasters pending. Metaphysics and Science techs will greatly increase your warning time. Authoritarian cultures that limit free speech and information will decrease your warning time. The interaction of these factors is complex. These are just examples.
    • Rarely, an artifact might have a destructive effect on a civilization
    • An unhappy population has increased probability of revolution and civil war
    • If hard science understanding advances faster than social science understanding, a runaway technology situation can occur where the civilization destroys itself
  2. Random visionaries may appear
    • The opportunity to advance your civilization can only be done when a visionary individual appears. You must invest T44 in that visionary immediately. The type of visionary will determine which area of tech your civilization advances in.
    • IMPORTANT: The frequency of visionaries appearing is proportional to the population of a planet. The higher the population, the more frequently a visionary appears. Half the population, half the Einsteins.
  3. Tritanium-44 bounty is dispensed. This is where the combined advances of your planet yield Tritanium-44 reward
  4. Players Invest T44 back into the planet's development
    • Raw materials can be converted into manufactured materials
    • Moons have natural resources but until the tech level achieves space travel, those resources are unknown. Exploiting them requires an even higher tech level and orbital infrastructure.
    • Trades can be initiated with other planets.
    • Acquiring predefined sets of planets and materials can yield Tritanium-44 production bonuses
    • Visionaries can be backed with T44 to give them the power and following to cause tech level, or cultural change
    • Improvements can be made to a planet such as adding a space elevator or other orbital infrastructure which will allow access to the resources on the moons of the planet.
