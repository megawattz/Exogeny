ExoPlaneteer is the NFT game where you can buy, sell, develop and trade planets and planet products.

After acquiring a planet, you can develop its tech level and population to acquire Tritanium-44, a crypto token tradeable with other crypto. You control the evolution of the planet by the choices you make to opportunities presented.

Multiple life forms, species, atmospheres, cultures, resources and more available on each planet make them all unique with their own strengths and weaknesses

The choices you make, and the responses you have to opportunities and disasters, determine how much Tritanium-44 crypto tokens your planet acquires per hour.
