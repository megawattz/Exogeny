The Planets of ExoPlaneteer

Planets are the GNFTs, Game NFTS, (available at http://opensea.com). Owning planets generates Tritanium-44 crypto tokens hourly.You can greatly increase that rate by advancing the planet's tech level and population. How this is done is by responding to opportunities presented every hour on your smartphone (or computer), that let you determine the development path of the planet, how it advances and how fast. The types of governments needed at specific times, which technologies to concentrate on, how much to spend salvaging disasters or investing in new technologies.

You can also increase the Tritanium-44 production rate by collecting sets of planets, for example

  • A set composed of planets from each type of life form
  • All the planets of a particular star
  • A set composed of every type of planet
  • Evolving an entire solar system to the same type of civilization

Planets have characteristics which affect their evolution and Tritanium-44 production.

Some things are intrinsic characteristics of a planet and cannot be changed (yet). These are planet_size, planet_terrain, clouds, atmosphere, official name like "Alpha Centauri X", galactic catalog number, planet index, moons, rings and a few other things.

Three things can be set immediately upon acquisition of a planet, or anytime the owner wants. These are:

  • Common Name - Like "Earth" as opposed to official name "Alpha Sol III"
  • Motto - Like, "The place where the rich come to enjoy themselves"
  • Species Name - What your civilization calls itself like, "Grungoids" or "Promilian", whatever want.

Characteristics

These are aspects of the planet that cannot be changed

Galactic Catalog Id - The unique identifier for this planet

Planet Terrain/Geomorphology - The type of planet, oceanic, gas giant, desert etc. Has a large effect on the events that occur to the planet, for example, desert planets cannot have tsunamis.

Atmospheric Composition - This affects what types of events can occur on the planet.

Moons - When a civilization unlocks space travel, moons can reveal new resources.

Official Name - How the planet is referred to in game generated actions, for example our planet would officially be named Alpha Sol III. "The 3rd planet in the first (alpha) star of the Sol star system". There is also a "common name" which the owner of the planet can set. Our planet's common name is, "Earth", in the English language.

Dominant Indigenous Life Form - This is the life form, the basic shape of the dominant species. It has a large effect on what the culture of the planet is capable of.

Life Chemistry - Has a large effect on events that can occur to the planet.

Resources

These are general categories of resources that are needed to create finished products for exchange with other planets. For example, the formula for "consumer products" might be 10 units of Industrials and 2 units of Specialized. The formula for a spaceship might be 20 units of Industrials, 1 unit of Radioactives, 1 unit of Refractories, 2 units of Specialized and Knowledge Level 2.

Industrials - Abundant materials

Rare Ores - Generically rare materials.

Radioactives - Naturally occuring radioactive materials needed to initiate nuclear reactions.

Refractories - Materials able to operate at high temperatures and pressures.

Specialized - Materials with unique properties for which there is no substitute.

Biologicals - Life and byproducts of life.

Exotics - Materials unique to the planet, think "Spice" of the Dune novels.

Relics - Technological artifacts which are very, vary rare but can give your civilization a fast boost in tech level

Wealth

These are the accumulated resources the population has created, not natural raw materials

Infrastructure - The buildings, roads, bridges, power plants, real estate, universities, transportation grid. The physical build up of structure to enable high population and technology

Manufactured - Manufactured items such as tools, vehicles, pharmaceuticals, consumer goods, weapons, computers etc etc

Services - The services that the population can provide to other civilizations such as research, intellectual property, assistance, counseling, education, etc. The non-physical assets.

Entertainment - Exported media, fiction and attention grabbing materials that other civilizations desire.

Population - The number of living creatures of the dominant species. This is im portant because the higher the population, the higher the Tritanium-44 production.

Tritanium-44 - The stockpile of Tritanium-44. The planetary reserves that have not been moved into a crypto wallet yet.

Knowledge - The accumulated research and discoveries of the culture.

Woo - The spiritual power of the culture. Intelligent life devoid of fingers and hands develops pure mental or PSI powers, which we will call "woo" for lack of a better word.
