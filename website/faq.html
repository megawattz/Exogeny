ExoPlaneteer Overview
  • NFT game for busy people who cannot sit in front of screen all day
  • This is not the game for hard core gamers, it's for casual players who cannot, or do not want to, sit in front of a screen in all their free time. It's also probably 1/100th of the effort required for a full blown, immersion, space shooter. This is more like buying and managing fictional, galactic real estate.
  • Planets are NFTs
  • Planets acquire crypto token Titanium-44
  • Planets actively acquire crypto and can be interacted with for entertainment
  • Simple game for underserved market of consumers with disposable incomes who don't have time to sit in front of computer playing games all day long. This offers a diversion and entertainment without monopolizing ones time.
  • Working name ExoPlaneteer, another possibility is ExoPlaneteer (to capture recent interest in Exo Planets)
  • See About for more information
What makes ExoPlaneteer different?
  • ExoPlaneteer NFTs are animations, not just images.
  • ExoPlaneteer NFTs actually acquire crypto. They don't just sit there like Boring Apes
  • ExoPlaneteer NFTs can be improved and customized
  • ExoPlaneteer NFTs actively entertain and the player can feel a sense of ownership because of the work put into the planet
  • Simple, easy to learn.

Game Play
  • Capture elements of simple games
  • Higher tech level, population and moral increase Titanium-44 production
  • Players can improve their planets by adding infrastructure like the all-time favorite game: Monopoly. Improved planets generate more Titanium-44.
  • Very simple, players allocate Titanium-44 (the game token) towards different areas of technology growth of the planet
  • Players can also collect sets of planets to increase Titanium-44 production like many card games (poker, gin rummy etc)
  • Elements of chance so the game is not overly dependent on a chess level intellect
  • Players allocate T44 towards tech or infrastructure, then at the top of the hour...
  • ...the game calculates results, crediting crypto wallets with Titanium-44 and alerting players to significant events for their planet(s), by email or SMS if the player wants.
  • Random events happen which can negatively or positively affect planet Titanium-44 production
  • Initially P2E but PVP is coming as soon as play balance is achieved
  • Important to preserve simplicity for new players and never overwhelm them with too much game detail until they are ready
